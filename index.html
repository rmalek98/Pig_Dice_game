Pig is a simple dice game which in its basic form is playable with just a single die. You win by being the first player to score 100 or more points.

- To palay the game, you need to roll the dice and then either hold or roll again.
- If you roll a 1, you lose all your points and it's the next player's turn.
- If you roll any other number, you add it to your turn total and it's the next player's turn.
- If you hold, you add your turn total to your score and it's the next player's turn.
